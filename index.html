About Us

Experience the ultimate in hospitality and personal service in the heart of Kyiv. Hotel Nikki, located steps from Peremohy Square, blends urban convenience with Eastern charm for a luxurious and boutique hotel experience. Surrounded by the bustle of Peremohy Square and sights of Polytechinc Institute, Hotel Nikko offers contemporary accommodations moments from the city's best, with on-site access to a world of dining, entertainment, and serenity.

Being opened in the end of 2011, "Hotel Nikki" immediately declared itself seriously at the hospitality market. Here are some of our awards and honors. The first award, "Crystal brick", "Hotel Nikki" received as the best investment project. Also, the hotel is included in the prestigious list of "100 Best Hotels of Ukraine", is a member of Five star Alliance, and a leader on Booking.com among Kyiv hotels. "Hotel Nikki" is recommended by the Tripadvisor - the world famous travel portal. Restaurant "Hotel Nikki" won in the category "Lions-gourmet" in the "Face of the City" competition. This autumn, "Hotel Nikki" won another prestigious ranking, becoming the best small hotel in Ukraine according to International Hotel Awards. "Hotel Nikki" is the only world class small hotel in Kyiv that provides a full range of services. Recognizable by its unique architecture, using hand painting and musical clock with man-tall figures, "Hotel Nikki" is included to guide tours as one of the attractions of the city.
